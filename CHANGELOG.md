## Release entry rules

For each release:

1. Move relevant items from `Unreleased` into a dated version section.
2. Group changes under Added, Changed, Deprecated, Removed, Fixed, and Security.
3. Link material changes to issues, pull requests, decisions, or evidence where available.
4. Describe user-visible or governance-relevant outcomes rather than individual edits.
5. Keep historical entries immutable except to correct factual errors.

## Versioning guidance

- **Major:** incompatible governance, structure, or normative framework changes.
- **Minor:** backward-compatible capabilities, standards, or review assets.
- **Patch:** corrections, clarifications, and non-breaking maintenance.

Pre-release identifiers such as `0.9.0-alpha.1` may be used while the framework is under active development.
