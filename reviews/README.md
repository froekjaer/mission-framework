## Review principles

Reviewers are invited to challenge the framework rather than confirm it.

Please distinguish clearly between:

- observations
- interpretations
- recommendations
- open questions

Canonical meaning belongs in this repository. Implementation findings should also be reflected in `Mission-Platform`, and real-world validation findings should also be reflected in `mission-solar-eclipse`.
