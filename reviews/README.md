## Review roles

The invitations deliberately emphasise different perspectives:

- **z.ai** — independent cross-system architecture challenge
- **Codex** — technical architecture, implementation boundaries and conformance testing
- **Claude** — conceptual coherence, semantics and editorial precision
- **Human reviewer** — practical judgement, accountability, usefulness and professional experience

These roles are starting points rather than restrictions. Reviewers should report any important finding regardless of category.

## Review principles

Reviewers are invited to challenge the framework rather than confirm it.

Please distinguish clearly between:

- observations
- interpretations
- recommendations
- open questions

Canonical meaning belongs in this repository. Implementation findings should also be reflected in `Mission-Platform`, and real-world validation findings should also be reflected in `mission-solar-eclipse`.

No review response becomes canonical merely by being submitted. Findings must be evaluated, discussed and incorporated through an explicit decision or pull request.
