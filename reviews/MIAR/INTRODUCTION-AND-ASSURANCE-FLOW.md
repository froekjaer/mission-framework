# Introduction and assurance flow

**Status:** Controlling transition note  
**Version:** 1.0

## Precedence

For first human contact, `ORGANISATION-INTRODUCTION-TEMPLATE.md` and each agent folder's `MIAR-ORGANISATION-INTRODUCTION.md` supersede any earlier wording that presents the recipient with an immediate review assignment.

Earlier invitation files remain historical working material until separately archived or revised. They shall not be used as the first-contact message where they conflict with this flow.

## Two distinct invitations

### 1. Focal AI invitation

The focal AI agent receives the MIAR assignment and performs the independent review. This is a substantive assurance task.

### 2. Human organisation introduction

The human recipient receives a soft introduction and an awareness package. This is not an assurance task and creates no expectation of review, endorsement or reply.

## Required sequence

```text
Focal AI completes and freezes MIAR Phase 1
                ↓
Focal AI verifies a suitable human recipient
                ↓
Focal AI completes the organisation introduction
                ↓
Human owner approves the text and generated artefacts
                ↓
Introduction and awareness package may be sent
                ↓
Dialogue, interest or questions
                ↓
Optional invitation to MIAR or another bounded review
```

## Awareness package

The first-contact package should contain:

1. a generated Project Extract presentation;
2. the generated Mission Framework book;
3. public repository and provenance references.

Every generated artefact shall identify version, generation date and canonical source commits. Draft or missing artefacts shall not be represented as available.

## Credibility discipline

The introduction shall apply the operational model in `docs/operational/TRUST-BOOTSTRAP-CREDIBILITY-ONBOARDING-EVIDENCE-MATURITY.md`.

In particular, it shall distinguish:

- the short public formalisation period;
- the longer history of professional experience and source material;
- current evidence maturity;
- AI assistance from human governance responsibility;
- interest from endorsement;
- model-generated review from provider participation.

## Review requirement

MIAR reviewers shall assess whether this two-step flow is accurate, non-deceptive, culturally adaptable and proportionate. They should recommend rejection or redesign if the awareness package overstates maturity or creates implied endorsement.
