# Academic Review Track

This track is intended for researchers and scholars in fields such as epistemology, philosophy of science, systems thinking, organisational learning, decision science, knowledge management, governance, human–AI collaboration and related disciplines.

Please pay particular attention to:

- conceptual novelty and overlap with existing literature
- precision and internal consistency of definitions
- separation of observation, hypothesis, normative principle and validated knowledge
- testability and possible falsification
- methodological adequacy
- generalisability and boundary conditions
- claims that require citations or empirical support

We welcome recommendations to replace Mission Framework terminology with better-established concepts where appropriate.

A useful academic review need not cover the full repository. A narrow, well-supported critique is valuable.
