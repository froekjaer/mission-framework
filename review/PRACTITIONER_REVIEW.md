# Practitioner Review Track

This track is intended for experienced practitioners in architecture, security, governance, programme delivery, critical infrastructure, public administration, healthcare, maritime operations and other mission-oriented domains.

Please assess:

- whether the framework helps clarify purpose, accountability and evidence
- whether its artefacts are proportionate to the value and risk involved
- where the method becomes impractical, slow or bureaucratic
- whether responsibilities and delegated authority are operationally clear
- which failure modes are missing
- what minimum viable implementation would be useful
- which existing standards or methods should be preferred or integrated

Concrete examples, counterexamples and descriptions of operational constraints are especially valuable.

The framework should be judged by whether it supports better decisions, better evidence and better learning—not by the elegance of its terminology.
