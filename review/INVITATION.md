# Invitation to Review Mission Framework v0.2

Mission Framework v0.2 is being released as an open working draft for independent review.

You are receiving this invitation because your expertise may help improve the framework.

Mission Framework explores whether Collaborative Intelligence can become a reproducible discipline for improving understanding through collaboration between people, artificial intelligence and reality.

The framework does not claim that this objective has been achieved. It proposes concepts, methods and governance structures that should be examined, criticised, refined or rejected.

We particularly welcome comments on:

- conceptual originality and overlap with existing disciplines
- hidden assumptions and unsupported claims
- definitions that are unclear, circular or unnecessary
- methodological weaknesses
- practical applicability and failure modes
- governance, accountability and ethical implications
- evidence required for validation

Participation should not be interpreted as endorsement of Mission Framework, its contributors or its conclusions.

We are not asking reviewers to agree. We are asking them to help us become less wrong.
