# Mission Framework Culture

> *Culture cannot be enforced. It can only be demonstrated.*

Mission Framework is built by people who choose to learn together.

This document describes the culture we strive to create and preserve.

## Curiosity

We ask questions.

We explore.

We challenge assumptions.

We remain willing to change our minds when new evidence appears.

Curiosity is the beginning of improvement.

## Humility

No one knows everything.

Every discipline has something to teach.

Every contributor has something to learn.

Humility allows knowledge to grow.

## Courage

We are willing to question accepted ideas.

We are willing to acknowledge mistakes.

We are willing to improve our own work.

Progress requires courage.

## Respect

We discuss ideas.

We do not attack people.

Disagreement is expected.

Disrespect is not.

Every contributor deserves to be heard.

## Evidence

Opinions begin conversations.

Evidence improves them.

Whenever possible, we seek observations, experience, research, and practical validation.

Reality has the final vote.

## Simplicity

Complexity can hide misunderstanding.

Simple explanations are difficult to create.

We value clarity over unnecessary sophistication.

Understanding is our objective.

## Collaboration

Mission Framework belongs to no single discipline.

It grows where different perspectives meet.

We encourage collaboration across professions, organisations, cultures, communities, and technologies.

## Stewardship

We do not own Mission Framework.

We care for it.

Every contribution should improve the Living Body of Knowledge for those who come next.

Stewardship is a responsibility, not a privilege.

## Openness

Ideas improve when they are shared.

Questions improve when they are welcomed.

Knowledge creates more value when it becomes accessible.

We choose openness whenever possible and responsible.

## Continuous Learning

Mission Framework is never finished.

Every mission teaches something.

Every contribution can improve something.

Every generation should leave something useful for the next.

## One Community

Some contributors will write.

Some will develop.

Some will research.

Some will review.

Some will test ideas in practice.

Some will ask difficult questions.

Every role matters.

## Our Culture

We learn.

We share.

We improve.

Together.

---

**Leave the work better than you found it.**
