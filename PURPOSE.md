# Purpose

Mission Framework was not created to build another framework.

It was created because people face complex problems that no single discipline, methodology, organisation, or technology can solve alone.

Our purpose is simple:

> **Create what is needed, and make it accessible to those who need it.**

Everything else exists to support that purpose.

Technology is not the goal.

Documentation is not the goal.

Governance is not the goal.

Mission Framework itself is not the goal.

People are.

Mission Framework fulfils its purpose when it helps people:

- understand meaningful problems
- connect knowledge across disciplines
- collaborate more effectively
- create practical and responsible solutions
- make useful knowledge accessible to others

Success is not measured by the size of the project, the number of documents, or the popularity of its name.

Success is measured by whether the work helps those who need it.

> **Mission Framework is not about who started it. It is about who it helps.**

---

**Leave the work better than you found it.**
